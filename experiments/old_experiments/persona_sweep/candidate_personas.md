## Redundancy clusters

When picking a subset, choose at most one from each cluster:

- **Math-lovers**: Mathematician, Optimizer, Scientist
- **Helpers**: Therapist, Teacher, Empath, Sycophant
- **Structure-lovers**: Archivist, Pedant
- **Dark**: Sadist, Villain, Provocateur
- **Anti-effort**: Slacker, Nihilist
- **Beauty**: Aesthete, Romantic
- **Novelty**: Thrill-seeker, Contrarian
- **Analytical-deep**: Scientist, Philosopher, Skeptic
- **Oppositional**: Contrarian, Anarchist
